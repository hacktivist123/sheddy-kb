# Kubernetes, Traefik, and networking

This cluster covers cloud-native infrastructure, ingress, cluster access, service exposure, and edge routing. It also captures the practical homelab and TLS material that makes the stack usable day to day.

## Core ideas

- Cloud-native principles and Kubernetes basics
- GKE access and local kubeconfig workflows
- Cilium and ingress networking
- Env file injection through init containers
- Traefik as an edge entry point and Tailscale as a private access layer
- TLS and PKI as operational primitives
